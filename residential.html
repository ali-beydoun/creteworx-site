Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a concrete driveway last?

A properly installed concrete driveway typically lasts 25-30 years with proper care. Key factors include quality of the concrete mix, proper base preparation, steel reinforcement, and regular sealing.

How long before I can drive on new concrete?

We recommend waiting at least 7 days before driving on new concrete. Walking is generally safe after 24-48 hours. Full curing takes approximately 28 days.

Do I need council approval for a crossover?

Yes, council approval is required for new crossovers or modifications to existing ones. We can assist with the application process for councils across Sydney.

What concrete finishes are available?

Options include plain grey, coloured concrete, exposed aggregate, stamped/patterned concrete, and honed finishes. Each has different aesthetic and maintenance characteristics.
